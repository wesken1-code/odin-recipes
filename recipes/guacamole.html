Guacamole

Guacamole Stock photos by Vecteezy

Description

Perhaps the easiest thing you will ever make. And yet, who can turn down some fresh guacamole?

This is a simple recipe, but you can add whatever you'd like to your guacamole to give it that special touch.

Ingredients

  • fresh ripe avocados
  • salt
  • garlic
  • lime juice

Steps

  1. Slice avocados in half from top to bottom and remove pit.
  2. Scoop flesh from skin (use a spoon to make it easier).
  3. Mash the yummy green flesh and add your spices and lime juice (the juice helps keep it fresh both in flavor and over time)
  4. Jump in with chips or put it in your tacos. Yum!
  5. Try not to eat it all before you can share with your friends and family.
