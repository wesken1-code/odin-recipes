Chicken Piccata

Chicken Piccata Stock photos by Vecteezy

Description

Want to impress someone with your Italian cooking skills? This dish is simple to make with just a handful of ingredients and it will keep you coming back for more.

Follow the simple steps below to get started and then you can perfect the recipe with you own personal flare.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (halved)
  • 1 fresh lemon
  • 1/2 Tbs olive oil
  • 2 Tbs of small Italian capers
  • 4 Tbs butter
  • 1/2 cup flour (sub gluten-free if desired)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat frying pan to medium-high heat, then add 1/2 Tbs olive oil
  2. Slice each chicken breast in half to make 2 thinner pieces
  3. Apply a thin coat of flour, salt, and pepper to both sides of each sliced breast
  4. Sear breaded breasts for 2-3 minutes each side until a you have a golden brown color on each side
  5. Squeeze lemon juice from lemon (you should have at least 2 Tbs
  6. After chicken is finished cooking, turn heat to medium-low and keep in pan. Add butter and lemon juice. The liquid will slowly thicken as it interacts with the flour from the coating on the chicken.
  7. Add oregano and cook on low stirring sauce over top of chicken to coat unitl sauce reaches your desired thickness. Then add your capers.
  8. Serve over your fvorite noodles and enjoy!
